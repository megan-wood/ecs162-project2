2048 Directions

To play 2048, press the arrow keys left, right, up, down to move the tiles in that direction. Try to move the tiles to combine tiles of the same numerical value to ultimately combine to reach 2048.

The game ends when the board is full and no more moves can be made to combine values of the tiles or if you have reached 2048. Good luck!
