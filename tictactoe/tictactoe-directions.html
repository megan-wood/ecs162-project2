Tic Tac Toe Directions

To play Tic Tac Toe, first choose a difficulty for the computer to play against. Then, click a square to place your move. Then, the comptuer will make a move and it becomes your turn again.

The game ends when the board is full, meaning there was a draw or one player won. A player wins when ther are three in a row of that player's piece on the board. A draw happens when there was no winnner.
